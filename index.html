HER 21ST · THE REAL PLAN

Night Before
Into Midnight

House party → midnight → bars/club · College Park · Budget: $150–250
The Night
7 PM
🍣 Sushi Boat House Party
Order from Wasabi Bistro and lay everything out on your table "boat" style — spread rolls across a long tray or cutting board with soy sauce dishes, chopsticks, and small plates. Looks way more impressive than it sounds. Add some snacks alongside.
  • Pick up, don't deliver — saves $5–8 in fees
  • Order 3–4 Maki Combos + individual rolls to fill the spread
  • Add edamame, gyoza as fillers — cheap and crowd-pleasing
  • Buy a big bag of rice crackers from H Mart as extra filler
8 PM
🎰 Casino Pregame
Dim the lights, put a gold/black tablecloth over the dining table, scatter some balloons and put on a casino/lounge playlist. Do poker with chips — whoever wins gets bragging rights. Keep drinks flowing. This is where the vibe builds.
  • Spotify: "Casino Royale Lounge" playlist is free
  • Dollar Tree has playing cards, balloons, plastic cups
  • Print a "21" banner free from Canva, tape it up
  • Do a birthday toast at 9 PM with everyone
11 PM
🥂 Get Ready Together
Everyone gets dressed, takes photos, hypes each other up. This is a whole vibe — do NOT skip it. Put on a hype playlist, set up a little photo spot with the "21" balloons in the background. Last drinks before heading out.
  • Take group photos here — lighting is better than the club
  • Birthday girl gets a sash + tiara — Dollar Tree has these
  • Have everyone's Uber ready to book at 11:45
12 AM
🎉 Midnight — It's Her Birthday
Head out at midnight so you literally arrive at the bar on her birthday. Do the countdown at your place and leave right after. Call the bar/club ahead and mention it's her 21st — many will do a free shot or free entry for the birthday girl.
  • Call ahead — "It's my girlfriend's 21st, what do you do for birthdays?"
  • Pre-book an Uber pool to split transport cost
  • Bring cash — some bars charge card minimums
  • Designate a person to track the group
2 AM
🌙 Wind Down
Back to yours or grab late night food on the way. Have a small cake ready at home — do the birthday song properly with candles. Way more intimate than doing it in a loud bar.
  • Grocery store cakes are $12–18 and totally fine
  • Have water and snacks ready — everyone will need them
